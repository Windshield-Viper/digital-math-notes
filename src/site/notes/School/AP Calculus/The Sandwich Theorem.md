#calc/theorem 
If $g(x) \leq f(x) \leq h(x)$ for all $x \neq c$ in some interval about $c$, and $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to c} g(x) = \lim_{x \to c} h(x) = L$:

Then $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to c} f(x) =L$.
